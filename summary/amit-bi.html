Project Details

🚀 Project Overview

This project, named AMIT BI, is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the performance of a business (likely a retail or e-commerce entity) over the years 2015-2017. The goal is to automate product data collection (revenue, profit, orders, and customer transactions) and visualize it into an interactive dashboard for further analysis. This project showcases data transformation skills.

This dashboard can be used by analysts, managers, or businesses to:

  • Track financial performance over time 📉📈
  • Compare product and customer performance 🔍
  • Collect structured data for market research 📊

🔧 Tech Stack & Libraries

Power BI 🖥️ Data Transformation 🛠️ Visualization 📊 Filters 🔍 CSV/Database 📑

📥 Data Extraction Workflow

Data Collection

Sourced from a structured dataset (likely CSV or database) containing sales, customer, and product details.

Data Transformation

Handled missing values and ensured consistency for accurate visualization.

Dashboard Creation

Designed multiple pages (Overview, Product, Customer Tooltip, Product Details) with:

  • ✅ Total Revenue and Profit
  • ✅ Customer Transaction Breakdown
  • ✅ Quantity Sold and Returned
  • ✅ Regional and Product Margin Analysis

Data Validation

Implemented filters and tooltips to manage missing elements, with debugging notes for refinement.

Save Results

Exported insights as interactive visuals and potential CSV exports for further analysis.

Columns include:

  • product_category
  • total_revenue
  • total_profit
  • total_orders
  • avg_order_value
  • returned_quantity
  • quantity_sold
  • margin
  • return_rate
