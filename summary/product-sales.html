This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze detailed sales performance across products, customers, and regions. Developed as of September 2025, it consolidates product sales data, customer stats, tax, freight, and order status into a dynamic, multi-page dashboard. The goal is to provide a clear, real-time view of key sales metrics to assist analysts and decision-makers in tracking business health and performance.

The dashboard includes data on 219 products and 93 customers, presenting total sales, tax, freight costs, and detailed customer purchase behaviors. It enables easy navigation between overview, product details, and customer-level insights.
