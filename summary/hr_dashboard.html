This HR Analytics Dashboard built with Tableau provides comprehensive insights into human resources data. The dashboard is designed to help HR managers make data-driven decisions by offering both high-level summary views and detailed employee records for in-depth analysis.

  • Summary View - High-level insights across Overview, Demographics, and Income Analysis

  • Department Breakdown: Total employees by department and job titles
  • Location Analysis: HQ vs branches comparison (New York as HQ)
  • Geographic Distribution: Employee distribution by city and state

üë• Demographics

  • Education Levels: Distribution of employees by educational background

  • Education Count: Total employees at each education level
  • Performance Correlation: Relationship between education and performance ratings

üí∞ Income Analysis

  • Age-Salary Correlation: Relationship between age and salary across departments
  • Departmental Salary Trends: How age correlates with salary in each department
